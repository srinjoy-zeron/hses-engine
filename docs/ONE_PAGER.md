### [The Problem]
**The Invisible Risk**
Cybersecurity tools defend networks, endpoints, and clouds, but they ignore the most critical attack surface: **The Human**.
*   **74%** of breaches involve the human element (Verizon DBIR).
*   Stress, fatigue, and distraction make even the best-trained analysts vulnerable to social engineering.
*   Security teams currently have **zero visibility** into the real-time cognitive state of their workforce.

---

### [The Solution]
**HSES: Real-Time Human Risk Intelligence**
The Human Security Exploitability System (HSES) bridges the gap between biology and security. By ingesting real-time physiological data and contextual metadata, HSES quantifies how "exploitable" a user is at any given second.

> "A fatigued admin at 3 AM is 10x more likely to click a phishing link than a focused analyst at 10 AM. HSES proves it."

---

### [How It Works]
**The 7-Step Scoring Pipeline**

1.  **Ingest Signals**: We collect non-invasive signals like heart rate, typing speed, jitter, and screen focus time.
2.  **State Analysis**: Our engine maps these signals to cognitive states: *Stress, Fatigue, Focus, or Confusion*.
3.  **Contextual Multipliers**: We adjust for environmental factorsâ€”Time of Day, Session Length, and Background Noise.
4.  **Risk Calculation**: The engine computes a raw "Exploitability Score" (0-100).
5.  **Scenario Prediction**: High scores trigger specific vulnerability warnings (e.g., *"High Vulnerability to BEC"*).
